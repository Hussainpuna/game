WORD PUZZLE

📖 How To Play

Guess the hidden 5-letter word in 6 tries. Each guess must be a valid word. After submitting, tiles change color:

• Green : The letter is correct and in the right spot.

• Yellow : The letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.

• Grey : The letter is not in the hidden word at all.

💡 Stuck? You can use 1 Green letter reveal and 1 Theme clue per game to assist your choices!
