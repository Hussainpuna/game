📖 Rules & Scoring Mechanics

Guess the hidden 5-letter word in 6 tries. Submit using the Enter key. Tiles will change color to provide clues:

• Green : The letter is completely correct and in the right spot.

• Yellow : The letter is in the word, but currently in the wrong spot.

• Grey : The letter does not exist in the hidden word at all.

🎯 Score Breakdown:

• Solve cleanly without hints: +50 pts

• Using Green Letter Hint penalizes final round score by -10 pts.

• Using Theme Clue Hint penalizes final round score by -20 pts.

• Round Failure / Loss / Skipped: Your total score drops based on used hints (defaults to -10 pts if no hints were pulled).
